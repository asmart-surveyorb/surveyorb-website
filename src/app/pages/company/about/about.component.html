About Us

We're on a mission to make feedback meaningful

Survey Orb was built to help organizations of all sizes collect, understand, and act on feedback.

Our Story

Survey Orb was founded on a simple observation: collecting feedback is easy, but getting actionable insights from it is hard. Traditional survey tools generate data, but leave you to figure out what it means.

We built Survey Orb to change that. By combining intuitive survey design with AI-powered analysis, we help teams move from data collection to decision-making faster than ever before.

Today, thousands of organizations use Survey Orb to understand their customers, engage their employees, and make better decisions based on real feedback.
